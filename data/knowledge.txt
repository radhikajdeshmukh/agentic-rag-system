Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that focuses on building systems 
that can perform tasks requiring human intelligence. These tasks include learning from data, 
understanding language, recognizing images, making decisions, and solving problems.

Unlike traditional programs that follow fixed rules, AI systems learn patterns from data 
and improve over time. Machine Learning is a major part of AI, where models are trained 
using large datasets to make predictions or classifications.

Common examples of AI include chatbots, recommendation systems, voice assistants, 
fraud detection systems, and self-driving car features. AI helps automate complex tasks 
and supports humans in making faster and smarter decisions.
